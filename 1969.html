HISTORY OF ARNIS

1969

In 1969, Mr. Remy A. Presas taught Arnis to some public and private school teachers inManila. He called his style as “Modern Arnis”, but this was his own personal style and not representative of contemporary Arnis. Mr. Presas also taught to the students of the National College of Physical Eduction (NCPE), and his students became proponents of his style when they became P.E. teachers themselves. This was the beginning of Arnis being taught in schools.
